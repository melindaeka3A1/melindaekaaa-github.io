Who We Are???

We are STIE University (Indonesian Economic College) in Pontianak and STBA University (Foreign Language College) Pontianak. we are a university with different majors. STIE Is the Foundation or legal entity that organizes the 'Indonesian' College of Economics Pontianak is the Indograha Management Education Foundation which was established on March 12, 1992 based on the Notarial Deed of Sri Rohani Wahyudi, S.H. No.34 and has been registered at the Pontianak District Court No.08/YYPENDAF/1992/Pn.PTK and has been approved by the Decree of the Minister of Law and Human Rights No. AHU 2276.AH.01.04 of 2010. Based on SK No.073/D/0/1998, the Minister of Education and Culture RIcq. Directorate General of Higher Education cq. Kopertis XI Depdikbud R.I., then established the Pontianak College of Economics (STIE) which provides education in the fields of Management (Bachelor Program/S1) and Accounting (Three Diploma Program/DIII). STBA Is a Foundation or legal entity that organizes the Pontianak Foreign Language College is an Indograha Management Education Foundation which was established on March 12, 1992 based on the Notarial Deed of Sri Rohani Wahyudi, S.H. No. 34 and has been registered at the Pontianak District Court No. 08/Y.Y.PENDAF/1992/PN.PTK and has been approved by the Minister of Law and Human Rights Decree No. AHU 2276.AH.01.04 of 2010. The Pontianak Foreign Language Academy / ABA was established in 1994 based on the Decree of the Director General of Higher Education of the Ministry of National Education & Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture No. 065/D/0/94 and organizes Diploma Program in English. In 2006, ABA Pontianak obtained an accreditation score of "B (Good)" BAN-PT, then ABA Pontianak again obtained an accreditation score of "B (Good)" BAN-PT in 2014. Based on the Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia No. 590/E/0/2014 dated October 17, 2014, ABA Pontianak was entrusted by the Director General of Higher Education to organize an English Literature Study Program (Bachelor Program/S1) and subsequently changed its form to a College of Foreign Languages ​​/ STBA Pontianak. Starting from 2015, STBA Pontianak officially organizes the English Literature Study Program (Bachelor Program / S1) and the English Language Study Program (Three Diploma Program / DIII)

These two universities are known as Universities that produce superior, independent, and multicultural human resources in the field of management and foreign languages ​​in Indonesia.
